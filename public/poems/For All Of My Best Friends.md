# For All Of My Best Friends

I know I haven't been around lately
I know I've been more physically distant than you all have ever been
Showing off myself in places you all could never dream
Living out my best life or so it would seem
But I think of y'all every day and I miss you dearly

Through my repeated drug use over the years
Through my sometimes oblivious drinking
I know you all worry about me, always thinking
You all say it through your "Be safe" and "Be careful"s
Little words that could mean so little, but they truly quiet my fears

It's our histories that have built us up
Not just the experiences we share
But the way we hold each other up
Making sure we all get there
Without one another, I know we wouldn't be enough

Through the countless stories I post
Finding ways to reach out to anyone reading
On and on about the existential loneliness I'm feeling
I know you all are there for me, cheering
That I survive another day, so we meet again, my friends

For all of my best friends
There's nobody that could replace any of you
For all of my best friends
I wouldn't want it any other way, it's true
For all of my best friends
I love you very, very, very much
And I only hope for the best for each and every one of you

I'll see you all soon
